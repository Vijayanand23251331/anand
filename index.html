Jule AI — Legal Intelligence Platform

At HackForge, I built Jule AI — an AI-powered legal assistant designed to transform how lawyers work. The platform leverages large language models to help legal professionals draft documents instantly, research case law, summarize lengthy contracts, and automate repetitive legal workflows.

The goal was simple: make legal work faster, smarter, and more accessible. Lawyers spend hours on documentation and research — Jule AI cuts that down dramatically, letting them focus on what matters most: their clients.
